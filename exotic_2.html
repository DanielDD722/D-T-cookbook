SPICY RED BEEF BOMB

29min preparation time

Amount Incredients
1/2 cube (21 g) yeast
1 tsp sugar
1/4 liter milk
1/8 liter waterr
50g butter
1 tsp to taste Red food coloring (e.g. Wilton Chrismas Red)
600g flour
1 tsp salt
1 egg (size M)
1 egg yolk
3 tbsp milk
1 tbsp sesame for sprinkling

DESCRIPTION

  1. For 8-10 pieces: Mix 1/ 2 cube (21 g) of yeast with 1 teaspoon of sugar until liquid. Heat 1⁄ 4 l milk, 1⁄ 8 l water and 50 g butter (add 1 tsp red food paste (e.g. "Christmas Red" from Wilton) if you like). 600 g flour, 1 tsp salt and 1 egg (size. M) in a bowl.
  2. Add the milk and yeast mixture. Knead everything with the dough hook of the mixer to a smooth dough. Cover and let rise in a warm place for about 45 minutes
  3. Knead the dough briefly, shape into 8-10 rolls and place on two baking trays lined with baking paper. Cover and leave to rise for about 20 minutes. Preheat the oven (electric stove: 200 °C/convection oven: 180 °C/gas: see manufacturer).
  4. Whisk 1 egg yolk with 3 tbsp milk. Brush the buns with it. Sprinkle with 1 tbsp sesame seeds. Bake in the hot oven for about 15 minutes one after the other.
