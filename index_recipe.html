SPICY DOUBLE BEEF WHOPPER

25min preparation time

Amount Incredients
1 tbsp vegetable oil
450g minced (ground) beef - ideally 20% fat
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp ground black pepper
4 slices burger cheese - use regular burger cheese or slices of your favorite cheese
2 brioche buns - toasted
large handful curly lettuce
2 large tomatoes
1/4 brown onion
8 tbsp Burger Sauce

DESCRIPTION

  1. Preheat a griddle or large frying pan until hot and brush with the vegetable oil.
  2. Form the beef mince into 4 patties and sprinkle on both sides with the salt and pepper.
  3. Place the patties on the griddle and cook, for about 3 minutes, until browned, then turn the burgers over and place a slice of cheese on top of each burger.
  4. Cook for a further 1-3 minutes, until the burger is cooked through.
  5. Cook for a further 1-3 minutes, until the burger is cooked through.
  6. Take the burgers off the griddle when cooked and leave to rest for 2-3 minutes.
  7. Whilst the burgers are resting, mix the sauce ingredients together and put to one side.
  8. To assemble the burgers. Place the lettuce on the bottom of two of the burger buns followed by slices of tomato. Add a burger to each bun.
  9. Top with onion slices and another burger.
  10. Drizzle over the sauce and place the top of the bun on each burger and serve.
